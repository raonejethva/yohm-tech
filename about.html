About Company

Let us be your trusted partner

Yohm Technology stands out as a premier job-seeking service, distinguished by our extensive nationwide searches within the United States and global tech hubs. Our mission is to identify the most suitable candidates for a wide range of technical job roles.

Leveraging our in-depth experience in international recruitment law, we extend reach beyond national borders to place qualified individuals with STEM OPT, H1B, and GC visas. What truly sets Yohm Technology apart is our pioneering combination of job search marketing and real-time interview services.

Our Mission

With a seasoned team of experts, we ensure candidates receive personalized guidance through every facet of their job search journey. We pledge to provide job seekers with a platform that not only secures opportunities but also bestows significant recognition upon their professional careers.

Our Vision

Yohm Technology is driven by a commitment to trust-based relationships. Our vision highlights unyielding dedication to excellence and authentic bonds. Having successfully placed over 870+ candidates in high-paying roles, our goal this year is to serve 1,000+ candidates globally.

Brand Promise

Our brand promise at Yohm Technology is to realize your ambitions. We are committed to unveiling your dream job, utilizing our industry knowledge to connect you with roles that complement your goals. Your aspirations take center stage, guiding our every step toward a fulfilling career for you.
